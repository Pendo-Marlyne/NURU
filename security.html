Security You Can Trust
Built In, Not Bolted On

Protecting patient data, financial flows, donor privacy, and institutional transactions — every second, every transaction.

Security is not a feature. It is our foundation — designed with privacy, integrity, and transparency at the core.

Our Security Philosophy

At Nuru, we handle the most sensitive data in healthcare: patient identities, medical records, financial transactions, donor intentions, and institutional flows. We treat every byte as sacred — security is embedded in our DNA, not added later.

Privacy First

We collect only what is necessary. Patient data is anonymized where possible and always encrypted.

Integrity Always

Every transaction is immutable and auditable — no alterations, no hidden changes.

Transparency by Design

Patients & partners see exactly what data is used, how, and by whom — full audit logs available.

Enterprise-Grade Security Layers

Data Encryption

  • • End-to-end encryption for all patient & financial data
  • • AES-256 encryption standard (military-grade)
  • • Encrypted databases at rest
  • • Secure data transmission (TLS 1.3)

Identity & Access Management

  • • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) mandatory
  • • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
  • • Secure login with biometric options
  • • Institutional account verification & approval

Financial Transaction Security

  • • Encrypted payment channels (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • • Secure M-Pesa & bank integrations
  • • Real-time fraud detection & transaction monitoring
  • • Anomaly alerts & automated holds

Healthcare Data Protection

  • • Secure record storage with limited access
  • • Admin logs for every data access & modification
  • • Patient confidentiality safeguards
  • • Donor anonymity & protection protocols

Compliance & Governance

  • • Kenya Data Protection Act (KDPA) compliant
  • • HIPAA-aligned architecture
  • • Ethical data usage policy
  • • Regular third-party audits & certifications

Architecture & Risk Management

  • • Cloud-based secure infrastructure (redundant backups)
  • • Continuous monitoring & vulnerability testing
  • • Incident response planning & real-time threat detection
  • • Zero-trust network architecture
